    Solar AC Distribution Box (ACDB) Overview

    The ACDB is designed to safely distribute AC power from the solar inverter to the load or the main grid. It provides protection to the system and connected equipment from overcurrent, short circuits, and voltage surges.

    Components of a Solar ACDB

  • Protect the system from overloads and short circuits.

  • Provide individual control over connected circuits.
